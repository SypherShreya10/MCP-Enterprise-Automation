Based on the problem statement requirements ("automate complex business processes"), missing critical operational models are:
1. INVENTORY/STOCK MANAGEMENT (ERP Core)
Missing Models:
product.product ⚠️ CRITICAL MISSING

Why you need it: The entire problem statement talks about "Can we fulfill 200 units of Product X?"
What it does: Stores all product information (name, price, type, category)
Without it: You can't answer "What products do we have?" or "What's the price?"
Priority: HIGHEST - This is fundamental to ERP

product.template ⚠️ CRITICAL MISSING

Why you need it: Product variants and master product data
What it does: Template for products (one template can have multiple variants)
Without it: Can't understand product structure
Priority: HIGH

stock.quant ⚠️ CRITICAL MISSING

Why you need it: The problem asks "Do we have inventory?" "Can we deliver 200 units?"
What it does: Actual inventory quantities by location
Without it: You have NO WAY to check stock levels
Priority: HIGHEST - Can't do inventory checks without this

stock.location ⚠️ IMPORTANT MISSING

Why you need it: "Check inventory at Warehouse A"
What it does: Warehouse locations, storage areas
Without it: Can't know where stock is located
Priority: HIGH

stock.move ⚠️ IMPORTANT MISSING

Why you need it: Track inventory movements, understand why stock changed
What it does: Records all inventory movements (incoming, outgoing, internal)
Without it: Can't track inventory history
Priority: MEDIUM


2. SALES/ORDER MANAGEMENT (ERP Core)
sale.order ⚠️ CRITICAL MISSING

Why you need it: Problem statement: "Can we fulfill AutoCorp's order for 200 units?"
What it does: Sales orders (customer orders)
Without it: You can't CREATE orders, check order status, or track sales
Priority: HIGHEST - This is the main business transaction

sale.order.line ⚠️ CRITICAL MISSING

Why you need it: Orders have multiple products
What it does: Individual line items in a sales order (product, quantity, price)
Without it: Can't see WHAT was ordered, just that an order exists
Priority: HIGHEST


3. PURCHASING (ERP)
purchase.order ⚠️ IMPORTANT MISSING

Why you need it: Problem mentions checking "supplier lead times", "material availability"
What it does: Purchase orders to suppliers
Without it: Can't check incoming materials from suppliers
Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH

purchase.order.line

Why you need it: Purchase order details
What it does: Line items in purchase orders
Priority: MEDIUM


4. MANUFACTURING (If MRP module installed)
mrp.production

Why you need it: Problem asks "check manufacturing capacity", "can we manufacture 150 units?"
What it does: Manufacturing orders
Without it: Can't check production schedule
Priority: MEDIUM (only if you have MRP module)

mrp.bom (Bill of Materials)

Why you need it: Know what materials needed to manufacture
What it does: Lists materials/components needed for production
Priority: MEDIUM (only with MRP)

Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH

account.payment

What it does: Payment records
Priority: MEDIUM
